## ü§ñ About the Robot

The Fast Robots course uses a small, high-speed RC car chassis equipped with:

- **Artemis Nano** microcontroller with Bluetooth LE
- **IMU** (Inertial Measurement Unit) for orientation sensing
- **ToF Sensors** (Time of Flight) for distance measurement
- **Dual motor drivers** for differential drive control
